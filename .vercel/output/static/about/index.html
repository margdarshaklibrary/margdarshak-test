Who We Are

Built for Students Who Want Real Skills

Based in the historic city of Darbhanga, Bihar, Margdarshak Shikshan Sansthan is committed to delivering practical, job-oriented education. We understand that standard degrees often leave a gap between textbooks and employment, which is why we build real skill-based pathways.

Our institute focuses on modern, in-demand industries. We offer pocket-friendly vocational courses, hands-on internship programs, highly flexible hybrid learning models, government-registered certifications, and complete career placement support.

To support sincere and dedicated learners, we offer a unique, industry-first opportunity: 90% attendance = 100% fee refund. We believe that dedication and consistency should be rewarded, making education virtually free for our most focused students.
