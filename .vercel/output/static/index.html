About Margdarshak

Empowering Bharat Through Skilled Education

Margdarshak Shikshan Sansthan is Darbhanga’s premier educational institute dedicated to bridging the gap between theoretical knowledge and real-world employment. With a curriculum engineered by industry experts, we provide students with hands-on practice in computerized accounting, programming, tax filings, and digital marketing.

Our unique hybrid learning infrastructure allows students to study in our state-of-the-art offline computer labs or attend live online classes, backed by our core mission: providing premium education accessible to everyone.
