Internship Overview

Gain practical tax computation experience. In this internship, you will help analyze individual income sources, verify Form 16 and Form 26AS, calculate tax under old vs new tax regimes, and draft personal income tax returns (ITR-1 and ITR-4).

schoolEligibility:Finance students, accountants, and aspiring financial planners seeking practical income tax filing skills.
